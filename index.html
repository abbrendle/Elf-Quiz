Lothlorien

Home of lady Galadriel and lord Celeborn, in Lothlorien you will find your home among its massive tees up high. The Fellowship of the Ring visited "Lorien" after escapeing the mines of Moria.

Rivendell

Home of lord Elrond and his daughter Arwen, in Rivendell your home is at the foot of the Misty Mountains. The Fellowship of the Ring was formed here and Aragon's sword Narsil was reforged as Andruil.

The Grey Havens

Also called Mithlond, the Grey Havens are the departure point for the Undying Lands and the city of Valinor. Gandlaf, Frodo, Sam, Bilbo, Gimli, and Legolas all left for Valinor according to Tolkein's works.

Mirkwood
